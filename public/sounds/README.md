# Background sound files

Put your background sound MP3 files in this folder, using these EXACT names:

- `ocean.mp3` — Ocean waves
- `rain.mp3` — Rain on leaves
- `bowls.mp3` — Tibetan bowls
- `white-noise.mp3` — White noise

They will be served from `/sounds/ocean.mp3`, etc.

Notes:
- The app works fine even if these files are missing — the option just won't
  play any sound until you add the matching file.
- Use gentle, loop-friendly clips. They play quietly and loop continuously.
- "Silence" is the default and plays nothing.
