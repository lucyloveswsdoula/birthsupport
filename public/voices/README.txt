AFFIRMATION VOICES — one recording per voice.

HOW IT WORKS
- Each voice is a SINGLE .mp3 file: one continuous recording of someone gently
  reading the affirmations.
- When a voice is chosen in Settings, that recording plays during a contraction
  (over the quieter background sound). It pauses between contractions and resumes
  next time. "None" = no voice.
- The app works fine if a file is missing — that voice just won't play.
